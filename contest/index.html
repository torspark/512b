  • We're giving away 2 spots in the 2016 El Sol Blind Draw 4s & Shrimp Boil Beach Volleyball Tournament to be held on May 21st, 2016.

How to Enter

There are three ways to enter the contest:

  1. Like any 512 Beach post from this week (4/18 – 4/22) on Facebook or Instagram. Each unique like earns you another entry.
  2. Comment on any 512 Beach post from this week (4/18 – 4/22) on Facebook or Instagram. Comment on each unique post to earn another entry.
  3. Join the 512 Beach Update to get 2 bonus entries.
    • If you've already joined the 512 Beach Update, just reply to the email we sent you on Tuesday to collect your bonus entries.

Note: Max of 2 entries per post — 1 entry for a Like & 1 entry for a Comment.

How the Winners Will Be Selected

  • You have until noon on Friday, 4/22/16 to enter the raffle.
  • Winners will be chosen via a random drawing on Friday afternoon.
  • This drawing will be recorded to ensure it's fair. :)

Eligibility Requirements

  • You must be in Austin, TX & able to play in the Tournament on May 21st, 2016 (at Zilker, Krieg, and/or Aussies).
  • Prize is non-transferrable. If you win, and you can't play in El Sol on May 21st, 2016, we reserve the right to pick a new winner.
