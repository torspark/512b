What to Expect

Everyone who participates in our events knows who our sponsors are. We don't spam anyone, instead we make sure you get thanked & mentioned properly. If you ask any of our players or fans — they can tell you exactly who helped make that event bigger & better (You!).

We treat our sponsors right. Check out what some of our other sponsors have to say. You'll get everything listed in the package of your choice, guaranteed.

If you want to look at any stats, just let us know, and we'll send you a breakdown. To give you an idea, each event reaches at least 6,000 people on Facebook, plus the few thousand hits we get to our site every month. We don't even invite anyone to our Facebook events. It's all done organically by our players and fans who invite their friends.

Each event has 60–120 participants, plus a couple hundred fans & supporters who come out to watch throughout the day. Not to mention the several thousand runners, swimmers, hikers, dog-walkers, soccer players, paddle-boarders, kite-flyers, and other active Austinites who pass by the Zilker Volleyball Courts every Saturday. Just imagine how many positive impressions you can make for your brand in a single day, on top of all the interactions via social media leading up to each event.

And, we're just getting started. We want to make sure you're getting your money's worth out of sponsoring 512 Beach as we grow these events to be bigger & better, together.

Side Note: When we aren't working on 512 Beach, our day job is running our boutique marketing consultancy, Torspark. This knowledge & experience is what really helps us ensure you get the most out of sponsoring an event.
