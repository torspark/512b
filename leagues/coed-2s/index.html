Courtesy Rule

If a guy serves the girl more than once during a given serving rotation, it will be an automatic sideout & point for the receiving team.

Guys can't serve the opposing girl at all when his team has game point (even if it's the first "slip" during his serving rotation).

There will be a line marked at the midpoint of each baseline. If a guy's serve lands fully on the opposing girl's side of the line, it will count as a serve to the girl. If any part of the ball mark is touching the midline, it does NOT count as a serve to the girl. This line is meant to prevent teams from having the girl take 75% of the court and claim all the middle serves in an attempt to take advantage of the rule.

This is not meant to be used as a strategy for either player. Just play & have fun. :)
