FAQ's

How does the ladder work?

  • 5 players per rung.
  • Play 4 games & ref one game within your rung each week.
  • Games will be played to 21, cap at 21.
    • Normally we hate caps, but it helps with this format to keep the point spreads even & give credit to closely contested games.
  • 1st & 2nd in your rung move up the following week. 4th & 5th move down. 3rd stays.

When are the games?

You'll play at either 5:30pm or 7:30pm every Sunday in August (6th, 13th, 20th, & 27th).

First week's schedule will be posted on Friday evening, August 4th.

The following weekly schedules will be posted by Wednesday night each week.

What are we playing for?

  • King: 512 Beach Prize + Dripping Springs Vodka
  • Prince: Dripping Springs Vodka
  • 3rd Rung: 512 Beach Prize

What if I can't make it one week? What's the sub policy?

You can find a sub once per season (4 weeks), & he can maintain your rank if he finishes top 3 in your rung that week.

If your sub finishes 4th or 5th, you'll drop 1 rung (same as if you had earned 4th or 5th).

If you need to have a sub a 2nd time in the same season, you'll automatically finish 5th & drop 1 rung for the following week.

The 3rd time you need a sub within a season will automatically drop you 2 rungs.

If you don't find a sub, you'll automatically drop to the bottom rung & have -3 points for every game the following week.

If you don't find a sub a 2nd time, you'll be kicked out of the ladder & replaced.

Basically, subs can't move you up, but they can help you hold onto your ranking one time. And if you repeatedly need subs, you'll start dropping further each time, but not have any point penalty.

If you ever don't find a sub, you get dropped to the bottom & receive a 3 point penalty per game, because you let down the rest of your rung...
