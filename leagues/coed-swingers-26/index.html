FAQ's

How does a Swingers League work?

This is a fun 4's format, where you sign up as a 2-person coed team. You'll stay with your partner all season, but play with two new teammates each game.

You'll play 3-4 games (with 3-4 different pairs) each night.

Playoffs are the last week of the season.

What are the rules?

Standard USAV Beach Volleyball Rules with the following modifications:

  • Block does not count as a touch.
  • 4 team pools: Games to 25, switch once at 13.
  • 5 team pools: Games to 21, switch once at 11.

Players are expected to have integrity & call their own faults. The refs are there as an extra set of eyes to help keep score & resolve any disputes.

What if I can't make it one week? What's the sub policy?

Please find a sub (or subs) & let us know. 8 other people are dependent on your team being there, including your teammates for the week.

Subs can only be used in the playoffs if they played for your team earlier in the season.

Can I play in the Coed 2's League, too?

Sure! We have both leagues set up so your schedules shouldn't conflict at all.

When/Where can I find the schedule?

Schedules will be posted on this page after registration closes (usually a day or two before the league starts).

Playoff Brackets will be posted on the Live Update that week.

What's the cancellation policy?

You need to cancel 7 business days before the league is scheduled to start in order to receive a refund (minus a $10 processing fee).

If a league doesn't make, everyone will receive a full refund.
