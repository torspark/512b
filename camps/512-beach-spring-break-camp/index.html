What to Expect

We want to make sure everyone gets enough reps, instruction, and attention, so we always limit each court to a maximum of 10 players. This is the best ratio you'll find in town. Feel free to look around, we'll wait...

...

Now that you're back, here's what you need to know.

The Logistics

  • The Summer Training Series runs for five weeks (June 8th – July 8th).
  • Each session will start at 8:00 am and last 90 minutes.
  • All training sessions are centrally located at Krieg Field Volleyball Courts (directions).
  • Max six to one player to coach ratio.
  • Max ten players per court.
  • Each package is limited to the first 10 players to sign up. They'll go quick, so act fast!

The Features

  • Every session will feature progressive drills focused on the specific mechanics & strategies of beach volleyball.
  • We'll put the players in competitive cauldron scenarios where athletes can implement their newfound skills in game-like situations.
  • Every player will receive a free official Wilson beach volleyball.
  • We'll give everyone drills they can work on at home individually with their new ball and with each other to maximize their improvement each week and after they've completed the Summer Training Series.
  • No tryouts. Players will both be divided into separate skill groups as well mixed together so the stronger players can work with the beginners to improve their own abilities as well as the beginners'.
  • If you already have a tournament partner, just let us know, and we'll pair you together for most drills. If not, we'll help you find one.
  • You can choose to train on Mondays, Wednesdays, or both days each week.
