More Details

What's the format of each session?

Sessions are 90 minutes each.

We'll work on specific skills for most of the session, ending with focused gameplay to give you an opportunity to execute everything you've been practicing each day.

We encourage you to get there a little early to unwind after work & warm up before we get started, so we can make the most of our time on the court. But, we'll still start with a low-intensity drill each time, incase you can't get there until right at 6:00.

How are the sessions customized? What skills are covered?

Each session will be tailored to those who attend based on Player Assessments & any questions/topics brought up during training.

We'll be working on a variety of skills (Passing, Setting, Attacking, Defense, Blocking, Serving, & Game Theory/Strategy), depending on who attends each session & what you need.

If you're looking for help with something specific, just let us know, & we'll try to work it into your next session.

Who qualifies as "BB/A+ & higher?"

Our goal is to keep this group strong and make sure nobody is holding the group back. If you've at least made it to the finals in an Austin BB or out of pool in an Austin A/Open, you qualify automatically.

We also reserve the right to let guys in who have the right skills to provide value to the rest of the group, but maybe haven't technically met the requirement above (because they don't play as many tournaments). We'll take these guys on a case by case basis.

If you have any questions about whether or not you qualify, just drop us a note, and we'll help you decide.

Is the net the right height at Bailey?

Yes! We bought some new hardware that allows us to rig the net at exactly the right height (or even higher). Many of you know that the net at Bailey is notoriously off/low, which is why we've been hesitant to run anything there. But that's no longer an issue for us, thanks to several hours calculating/measuring & wandering around the hardware store...

What do you mean by "Instant Video Feedback"?

When the structure of a drill allows, we set up a video feed on a 10-second loop, so you can actually see what you just did. We've found that this helps players truly recognize & improve their mechanics, rather than just going through the motions.

How many players?

We only have one court, so we're capping it at 6 men per session to ensure you get enough quality touches & feedback.

Can I mix & match Volleyball and Performance Sessions?

Sure. If you buy either of the multi-packs (6 or 12 sessions), feel free to use them however you like.

For example, say you bought a 6-pack, but you can only make it to 2 of the volleyball sessions. You could then come to 4 performance sessions to use the rest of your 6-pack.

Just let us know which sessions you'll be attending each day.

What if a session is full? Or I need to reschedule?

If a session is already full, we'll be in touch to reschedule or give you a refund.

If you need to reschedule a session, just let us know at least 24 hours in advance. This gives us time to offer your spot to our waitlist and/or change the practice plan.
