Format

Double-Elimination Bracket w/ Matches throughout. Full matches are best of 3, with games to 21, 21, 15. No cap. Mini-matches (best of 3 games to 15, no cap) may be used in some of the Loser's Bracket during larger tournaments, due to time, heat, & court constraints.

Why is the Start Time TBD?

We sometimes stagger the start times, depending on the number of teams in each diviison.

This helps ensure everyone's schedule runs smoothly.

We generally plan on an 8:30am check-in, & should be into the finals by about 5:30pm for most divisions.

Occaisionally, some larger divisions may run as late as 8 or 9pm.

The earliest we ever ask players to be on the court, ready to play is at 8am for some larger divisions. Schedules will be announced the night before if that's the case, so not everyone has to be there that early.

The latest check-in time we might use is 12:30pm for some smaller divisions.

Check-in times will be posted by 7:30pm the day before the tournament.

Payment

You need to pay in advance to hold your spot. If you have not paid by noon on the Wednesday before the tournament, we'll start going down the waitlist.

We accept Venmo & most major credit cards. When you click on either payment button below, you'll be redirected to a secure transaction page.

Venmo @FiveOneTwoBeach ($60)
Pay Entry Fee ($60)
Cancellation Policy

We have a 48 hour cancellation policy. You'll receive a full refund as long as you cancel at least 48 hours before the start of the tournament.

Other Ramblings

If you have any thoughts or suggestions on how we can make these tournaments bigger & better, please let us know.

If you can't make it to this tournament, but you'd like know when we run the next one, join our update list, and/or follow us on Facebook or Twitter. Instagram is where we'll share pictures & videos during the tournament.

We hope to see you in the sand!
