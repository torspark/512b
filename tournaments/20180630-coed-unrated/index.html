Format

Pool Play + Playoffs. Games to 21, no cap. Pool play will be used to seed top 3 or 4 teams from each pool in a playoff bracket. Playoffs will be games or matches, depending on time.

Who qualifies for this level?

New Rating System!

As a general guideline, your Rating Points + your partner's Rating Points should add up to less than the Team Rating Points Cap for this level.

You can see where you and your partner currently stand by checking your 512 Beach Points.

Note: Rating Points are meant to be a guide. That means you (or your partner) should NOT sandbag, just because one of you has fewer points than you know reflects your skill, for whatever reason.

We reserve the right to break up a team that seems too strong, but this is primarily an "on your honor" system.

If you're questioning whether or not you should play this level, check with your peers. Or, feel free to ask us.

Note: We also reserve the right to exlude any obviously sandbagging teams from advancing to playoffs.

Will there be a Courtesy Rule?

It depends on the level.

BB & Upper/Lower divisions will have a courtesy rule by default. All other divisions will not (B, A, AA, Open).

What is the Courtesy Rule?

If a guy serves the opposing girl's half of the court more than twice in a game or on game point, it will be an automatic point for the other team.

Teams have the option to not play with a courtesy rule on a game-by-game basis, if both teams agree beforehand.

Note: Serves down the middle are perfectly legal. Also, if the girl is pinching and takes a serve in the middle or on the guy's half, it does NOT count as a serve to the girl. She chose to take it.

Please don't try to game the system. We encourage you not to use this as a strategy. Just have fun & play within the spirit of the game. :)

Why is the Start Time TBD?

We sometimes stagger the start times, depending on the number of teams in each divison.

This helps ensure everyone's schedule runs smoothly.

We generally plan on an 8:30am check-in, & should be into the finals by about 5:30pm for most divisions.

Occaisionally, some larger divisions may run as late as 8 or 9pm.

The earliest we ever ask players to be on the court, ready to play is at 8am for some larger divisions. Schedules will be announced the night before if that's the case, so not everyone has to be there that early.

The latest check-in time we might use is 12:30pm for some smaller divisions.

Check-in times will be posted by 7:30pm the day before the tournament.
