Reverse Coed Upper/Lower Results

Format

Pool Play + Upper/Lower Playoffs. Games to 21, no cap. With 24 teams, if we finish pool play by 4pm, all teams come out of each pool to seed an upper & lower playoff bracket. Playoffs will be games or matches, depending on time.

Who can play in this tournament?

Open to all levels. :)

Prizes

24 teams
  • 1st: $400
  • 2nd: $150
  • LB: Prizes for 1st & 2nd
16+ teams
  • 1st: $200
  • 2nd: $100
  • LB: Prizes for 1st & 2nd
12+ teams
  • 1st: $100 + Prizes
  • 2nd: $50 + Prizes
  • LB: Prizes for 1st & 2nd
8+ teams
  • 1st: Prizes
  • 2nd: Prizes
* LB = Lower Bracket

Other Ramblings

If you can't make it to this tournament, but you'd like know when we run the next one, join our update list, and/or follow us on Facebook or Twitter. Instagram is where we'll share pictures & videos during the tournament.
