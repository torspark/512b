Format

Pool Play + Playoffs. Pools of 6, top 3 come out of each to seed a single-elimination bracket. Quarterfinals played to 21, Semis to 25, and a match for the finals.

No cap. Ever.

What does BB/A mean?

This is meant to be a fairly competitive tourney. That being said, everyone is welcome to come out and compete.

This is not a "High/Low." One player does not have to be definitively at the BB level. It's about the team. Austin's volleyball scene has grown to need a true A and/or AA tier to work your way up to AAA/Open. This is essentially an A or AA by CBVA standards. We just can't call it that, yet, because of how levels have been defined locally.

One rule: A/Open players, you can't play with anyone whom you've made it to the semis or better in an A/Open tourney anytime recently and/or would be picked by your peers to make the semis in an A/Open. This is an on your honor type of rule. Please, don't try to game it. For example, even though Joan Beltran & Andrew Russell haven't played together before, most people would pick them to make at least the semis in an Open. Therefore, they can't play together in this tournament.

Check-in

Everyone must be on-site for the player's meeting at 8:45 am. If you are late, your team will lose a point a minute for your first game.

There will be a brief player's meeting and a 10 minute warmup, so the first serves are in the air by 9:00 am. If you aren't playing on time, you'll lose a point a minute.

There aren't any lights at Zilker, and we don't cap any games, so we need to be strict about warm up times. 10 minutes on the court for your 1st game of the day. 5 minute warmup for every game thereafter.

Cancellation Policy

We have a 48 hour cancellation policy. You'll receive a full refund as long as you cancel before 9:00 am on Thursday, August 27th, or if you're able to find a team to replace your spot.

If you're wondering why the price went up from $25 to $30, it's so we can start taking credit cards and work toward making these tournaments bigger and better. Also, note that there won't be an additional credit card processing fee. $30 flat.
