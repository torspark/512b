Men's BB Mini-Series Stop #2
Moontower
August 27th, 2016

1st Place

Trevor Callarman & Matt Cisneros take 1st place at the Men's BB Mini-Series Stop #2 on August 27th, 2016. Undefeated on the day, but they provided plenty of excitement, with Trevor bouncing one into the creek & setting butter all day for Matt to swing away. They also showed how to win close games, coming out on top of their last 5 games: 31-29, 23-21, 25-23, 21-15, 23-21. Their opponents had advantage at the end in 4 of those games, but Matt & Trevor kept their composure & pulled off the win each time. #1stplace #512beach #mensBBseries #BeachVolleyball #tournament

Mini-Series Details

All players will earn USAV points for each tournament played.

Players who finish 1st, 2nd, & 3rd overall across the three stops will win additional prizes:

  • 1st: Monster Rockin' Roller Bluetooth Speaker
  • 2nd: Altec Lansing Mini H2O Bluetooth Speaker
  • 3rd: Wilson Beach Volleyball

* Need at least 15 teams at each stop to guarantee these prizes.

Format

Pool Play + Playoffs. Games to 21, no cap. Top 3 come out of each pool to seed a playoff bracket. Playoffs will be games or matches, depending on time.

Who qualifies as Men's BB?

We reserve the right to break up a team that seems too strong, but this is primarily an "on your honor" system.

If you're questioning whether or not you should play this level, you should probably just play in the Men's Open on on August 6th instead. Or, feel free to ask us.
