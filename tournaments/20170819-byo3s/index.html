Men's A Results

Format

Sign up with your own team of 3. Each team must have at least one girl.

Pool Play + Playoffs for the top 3 or 4 teams per pool, depending on time & number of teams.

We'll be playing by 2's rules (aka: hands need to be clean & block does count as a touch).

Who can play?

This format is open to all levels. :)
