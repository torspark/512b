Teams

We can take 12-18 teams, depending on how soon you guys sign up. We're running a Women's BB at the same time, so whichever gender gets to 15 teams first will get the 3rd court. The other gender will be capped at 12 teams.

It's up to you if you want a bigger tournament, you just have to get 15+ teams to sign up before the girls do. They have at least 9 teams so far. (Hint: It might help if you share this page on Facebook).

Format

It's up to you. When you register, your team will get to vote on the format.

Option A: Pool Play + Playoffs. This would be similar to most Aussies or TVT BB Tourneys. Pools of 6, top 3 come out of each to seed a single-elimination bracket. Quarterfinals played to 21, Semis to 25, and a match for the finals.

Option B: Double-Elim w/ Full Match Play. This would be a full double-elimination bracket with matches all the way through. Daylight permitting, we won't even have the "Olympic Crossover" for the semis. You have to lose two matches to get knocked out. We'd have to start an hour earlier in order to fit all these matches in. So, the check-in deadline would be 7:45 am instead of 8:45 am.

What does BB/A mean?

This is meant to be a fairly competitive tourney. That being said, everyone is welcome to come out and compete.

One rule: A/Open players, you can't play with anyone whom you've made it to the semis or better in an A/Open tourney anytime recently. This is an on your honor type of rule. Please, don't try to game it. For example, just because Tim Wooliver & Daniel Lindsey haven't played together for a few years, that doesn't mean they could get the band back together to play this tourney.

Check-in

Everyone must be on-site for the player's meeting at 8:45 am. If you are late, your team will start down 0-2 for all of pool play (or for your first two rounds of a double-elim bracket).

Please, try to get there a little early, because we'll ask you to sign a simple liability waiver as well. Then, we'll have a brief player's meeting and a 10 minute warmup so the first serves are in the air by 9:00 am sharp. If you aren't playing on time, you'll lose a point a minute.

There aren't any lights at Zilker, so we have to be strict about warm up times. 10 minutes on the court for your 1st game of the day. 5 minute warmup for every game thereafter.

Payment

At least one person from each team needs to pay in advance to hold your spot. If neither of you have paid by noon on the 23rd, we'll start going down the waitlist.

The best way to pay is with Square Cash through the button below. Square Cash requires you use a debit (NOT credit) card. This lets us avoid transaction fees in order to keep costs down. If you haven't tried it yet, it's pretty slick. We'll also take cash or check if you see us before the tournament or on-site. (But remember, at least one of you needs to pay in advance to hold your team's spot).

Cancellation Policy

We have a 48 hour cancellation policy. You'll receive a full refund as long as you cancel before 9:00 am on Thursday, July 23rd.

Why we're running this tournament

Basically, you asked us to...

Several of you heard about the Women's BB tourney we were approached about running, and asked if we could do something for the men, too. So, that's what we're trying to offer. We picked this date to give us time to prepare and to work around the other tourneys in town, because we're trying not to step on any toes. (There's a coed B at Aussies, and nothing on TVT's calendar or Rich's schedule).

Honestly, we're expecting to lose some money on this tournament. This is essentially an experiment to see if we're any good at running tournaments, and if you'd like us to run more in the future. If the answer is yes to both of those questions, awesome! If not, then at least we'll have had a fun day hanging out at Zilker.

If you can't make it to this tournament, but you'd like to know if we run any more in the future, join our mailing list, and/or follow us on Facebook or Twitter.

We hope to see you there!
— Chris & Zack (512 Beach)
