Teams

We can take 12-18 teams, depending on how soon you ladies sign up. The men were a little jealous, so we're running a Men's BB/A at the same time. Whichever gender gets to 15 teams first will get the 3rd court. The other gender will be capped at 12 teams.

It's up to you if you want a bigger tournament, you just have to get 15+ teams to sign up before the guys do. They have at least 13 teams so far. (Hint: It might help if you might help if you share this page on Facebook).

Format

Everyone is guaranteed at least 5 games. This tournament will be some form of pool play plus playoffs, but the specific formats will depend on the number of teams. For example, if we end up with just 8 teams, we'll run two pools of 4 (3 games per team) to seed an 8-team single-elim bracket with full matches throughout playoffs (daylight permitting). Our goal is to get you as many quality games as possible.

  • $100 cash*
  • Joico Gift Bags
  • Deschutes Brewery Hydroflasks
  • Deschutes Brewery T-shirts
2nd place

Joico & Deschutes Brewery are providing swag & samples for all players, too!

We're also taking song requests for our Pandora Tourney Quick Mix station, so you'll at least get to listen to music you like while you play, even if you don't win.

Check-in

Everyone must be on-site for the player's meeting at 8:45 am. If you are late, your team will start down 0-2 for all of pool play.

Please, try to get there a little early, because we'll ask you to sign a simple liability waiver as well. Then, we'll have a brief player's meeting and a 10 minute warmup so the first serves are in the air by 9:00 am sharp. If you aren't playing on time, you'll lose a point a minute.

There aren't any lights at Zilker, so we have to be strict about warm up times. 10 minutes on the court for your 1st game of the day. 5 minute warmup for every game thereafter.

Payment

At least one person from each team needs to pay in advance to hold your spot. If neither of you have paid by noon on the 23rd, we'll start going down the waitlist.

The best way to pay is with Square Cash through the button below. Square Cash requires you use a debit (NOT credit) card. This lets us avoid transaction fees in order to keep costs down. If you haven't tried it yet, it's pretty slick. We'll also take cash or check if you see us before the tournament or on-site. (But remember, at least one of you needs to pay in advance to hold your team's spot).

Pay $25
Cancellation Policy

We have a 48 hour cancellation policy. You'll receive a full refund as long as you cancel before 9:00 am on Thursday, July 23rd.

Why we're running this tournament

Basically, you asked us to...

Several of you asked for a good women's BB tourney, so that's what we're trying to offer. We picked this date to give us time to prepare and to work around the other tourneys in town, because we're trying not to step on any toes. (There's a coed B at Aussies, and nothing on TVT's calendar or Rich's schedule).

Honestly, we're expecting to lose some money on this tournament. This is essentially an experiment to see if we're any good at running tournaments, and if you'd like us to run more in the future. If the answer is yes to both of those questions, awesome! If not, then at least we'll have had a fun day hanging out at Zilker.

If you can't make it to this tournament, but you'd like to know if we run any more in the future, join our mailing list, and/or follow us on Facebook or Twitter.

We hope to see you there!
— Chris & Zack (512 Beach)
