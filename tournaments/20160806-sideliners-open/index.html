Teams

We're also running a Men's BB, so the number of teams for each division will be capped in multiples of 6, depending on how soon each division starts to fill.

In other words, if the Men's BB start to fill faster, then there will be fewer spots left for the Opens. So, don't wait too long to register. ;)

Format

Double-Elim w/ Match Play. This is a double-elimination bracket with matches all the way through.

Matches will be played to 21–21–15 in the winners bracket. Depending on the number of teams, some loser's bracket matches may be mini-matches (all 3 games played to 15). No Cap. Ever.

Seeding. This tournament will be seeded straight off of points listed on USA Beach Ranking as of Thursday, August 4th. No wildcards will be used. If you think you're missing some points, feel free to reach out to us, and we'll try to help you get them updated.

You'll also earn USAV points through this tournament. Assuming there are enough teams, this should be a 60-point-based tournament.

These points are useful for anyone traveling to play elsewhere (Seaside, Motherlode, etc.).

Who qualifies for Open?

This one's pretty easy to explain. No special rules for this tournament.

Open to anyone and everyone. Whether you hope to be in the money or just want to test your skills against some of the best around, you're welcome to come out and play.

Payment

Your team needs to pay in advance to hold your spot. If you have not paid by noon on Thursday, August 4th, we'll start going down the waitlist.

Cancellation Policy

We have a 48 hour cancellation policy. You'll receive a full refund as long as you cancel before 9:00 am on Thursday, August 4th, or if you're able to find a team to replace your spot.

Other Ramblings

If you have any thoughts or suggestions on how we can make these tournaments bigger & better, please let us know.

If you can't make it to this tournament, but you'd like know when we run the next one, join our update list, and/or follow us on Facebook or Twitter. Instagram is where we'll share pictures & videos during the tournament.

Some of the matches will be live streamed on our Facebook Page, if you'd like to watch.

We hope to see you in the sand!
