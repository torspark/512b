Teams

We can take 12 – 48 teams per gender, depending on how soon your gender signs up.

This is meant to be a Wide Open tournament, first & foremost. However, we don't want the courts to go to waste. Therefore, if signups are slow, we reserve the right to cap each division at the next multiple of six and open up the remaining courts to lower levels any time after Wednesday, June 1st.

Note: Both players need to register, so they can each agree to the liability waiver. Plus, this way everyone can request a song, too. :)

Format

Double-Elim w/ Full Match Play. This is a full double-elimination bracket with matches all the way through. We hope you're in shape. ;)
Matches will be played to 21–21–15. No Cap. Ever.

Seeding. This tournament will be seeded straight off of points listed on USA Beach Ranking as of Thursday, June 16th. No wildcards will be used. If you think you're missing some points, feel free to reach out to us, and we'll try to help you get them updated.

You'll also earn more USAV points through this tournament. Assumming there are enough teams, this should be a 90-point-based tournament.

All players will earn NVL points as well.

Two Days. If we have enough Open Teams (~24+ per gender), some matches will carry over to Sunday. If we fill at 48 teams in both genders, we plan to be done playing by 9pm on Saturday, & 5pm on Sunday (at the latest).

Player's Choice. If the Men's and Women's Opens don't fill, we'll run a Player's Choice tournament (or two) to make use of the courts. Vote for the format you want to play, & we'll let you know if/when registration opens.

What does Open mean?

This one's pretty easy to explain. No special rules for this tournament.

Open to anyone and everyone. Whether you hope to be in the money or just want to test your skills against some of the best around, you're welcome to come out and play.

In addition to all the cash, the winners will get Sponsor Swag (TBD), and all the women in the tournament will receive Joico product as well.

We're also taking song requests for our Pandora Tourney Quick Mix station, so you'll at least get to listen to some music you like while you play, even if you don't win.

Check-in

If everyone pays in advance, we'll send out the bracket Friday night and give you your own start time. This means a lucky few of you will get to sleep in a little.

Note: Some of you will still need to be there early to ref the first round of matches, even if you aren't playing until later. Please, check the schedule carefully. If your team is scheduled to ref, you're welcome to send just one team representative, but if you skip your reffing responsibility, you'll have to forfeit your first set to make up for the time lost.

First serves should be in the air by 8:00 am. If you aren't playing on time (whether that's 8:00 am or your personal start time), you'll lose a point a minute.

We're planning to play full matches all the way through the losers bracket, and we don't cap any games, so we need to be strict about warm up times. 10 minutes on the court for your 1st game of the day. 5 minute warmup for every game thereafter. Same "point a minute" rule if you're taking too long. Please, don't make us be the bad guy.

Payment

At least one person from each team needs to pay in advance to hold your spot. If neither of you have paid by noon on Thursday, June 16th, we'll start going down the waitlist. And again, everyone needs to pay for us to send out the brackets on Friday night.

We accept most major credit cards. When you click on the payment button below, you'll be redirected to a secure transaction page. You can choose to pay for the team or just yourself and have your partner pay their half.

Cancellation Policy

We have a 48 hour cancellation policy. You'll receive a full refund as long as you cancel before 9:00 am on Thursday, June 16th, or if you're able to find a team to replace your spot.

Other Ramblings

If you have any thoughts or suggestions on how we can make these tournaments bigger & better, please let us know.

If you can't make it to this tournament, but you'd like know when we run the next one, join our update list, and/or follow us on Facebook or Twitter. Instagram is where we'll share pictures & videos during the tournament.

We also post our Tournament Tracker update to Facebook every Monday, if you want to know about all the other tournaments going on around the area on each weekend.

We hope to see you in the sand!
