Format

Open

Double-Elim w/ Full Match Play. This is a full double-elimination bracket with matches all the way through. We hope you're in shape. ;)
Matches will be played to 21–21–15. No Cap. Ever.

Seeding. This tournament will be seeded straight off of points listed on USA Beach Ranking as of Thursday, June 16th. No wildcards will be used. If you think you're missing some points, feel free to reach out to us, and we'll try to help you get them updated.

You'll also earn more USAV points through this tournament. Assuming there are enough teams, this should be a 90-point-based tournament.

All players will earn NVL points as well.

Women's B

Pool Play + Playoffs. Games to 21, no cap. Top 3 come out of each pool to seed a playoff bracket. Playoffs will be games or matches, depending on time.

What does Open mean?

This one's pretty easy to explain. No special rules for this tournament.

Open to anyone and everyone. Whether you hope to be in the money or just want to test your skills against some of the best around, you're welcome to come out and play.

Who qualifies for the Women's B?

As long as you haven't won a BB (or higher) tournament, you're welcome to play in this tournament. If you're questioning whether or not you should play this level, you should probably play in the Open. Or, feel free to ask us.

Check-in

If everyone pays in advance, we'll send out the bracket Friday night and give you your own start time. This means a lucky few of you will get to sleep in a little.

Note: Some of you will still need to be there early to ref the first round of matches, even if you aren't playing until later. Please, check the schedule carefully. If your team is scheduled to ref, you're welcome to send just one team representative, but if you skip your reffing responsibility, you'll have to forfeit your first set to make up for the time lost.

First serves should be in the air by 8:00 am. If you aren't playing on time (whether that's 8:00 am or your personal start time), you'll lose a point a minute.

We're hoping to play full matches all the way through the losers bracket, and we don't cap any games, so we need to be strict about warm up times. 10 minutes on the court for your 1st game of the day. 5 minute warmup for every game thereafter. Same "point a minute" rule if you're taking too long. Please, don't make us be the bad guy.

Payment

At least one person from each team needs to pay in advance to hold your spot. If neither of you have paid by noon on Thursday, June 16th, we'll start going down the waitlist. And again, everyone needs to pay for us to send out the brackets on Friday night.

We accept most major credit cards. When you click on the payment button below, you'll be redirected to a secure transaction page. You can choose to pay for the team or just yourself and have your partner pay their half.

Cancellation Policy

We have a 48 hour cancellation policy. You'll receive a full refund as long as you cancel before 9:00 am on Thursday, June 16th, or if you're able to find a team to replace your spot.
